Groundwork

Run a loop. Claim the land.

Groundwork turns outdoor running into a live territory game. Track a loop, close it near where you started, and the space you enclosed becomes yours on the map.

What it does

Live run tracking, loop-based territory claims, rival takeovers, streaks, records, and a social feed.

How identity works

Groundwork uses your iCloud account through CloudKit. No email sign-up and no separate password.

Built for launch

V1 ships without ads, third-party analytics, or in-app purchases.

Core features

  • Outdoor route tracking with pace, distance, time, cadence, and elevation
  • Loop closure and territory claiming on a live map
  • Push alerts when another runner takes your land
  • Records, streaks, achievements, and rivalry leaderboards
